# Governance

platform-go is maintained as a reusable, business-neutral platform foundation.
Maintainers are responsible for architecture, security, release quality and
compatibility decisions. Contributors are welcome to propose changes through
issues and pull requests.

Changes to public contracts, authorization, persistence, migrations, release
automation or security policy require maintainer review and appropriate tests.
The maintainers may defer, reject or request a narrower change when it would
couple the foundation to a particular business domain.

Releases follow Semantic Versioning and the public guidance under [`docs/`](docs/)
and the repository's release metadata.
