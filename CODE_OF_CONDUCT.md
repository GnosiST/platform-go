# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a welcoming, respectful and harassment-free community.
Participants must not discriminate, harass, threaten, dox or otherwise disrupt
the project or its contributors.

Report unacceptable behavior privately to the repository maintainers through
the security contact configured on GitHub. Reports will be handled
confidentially and fairly. Maintainers may remove comments, restrict
participation or take other action needed to protect the community.

This policy is adapted from the Contributor Covenant and applies in project
repositories, issues, pull requests, discussions and project events.
